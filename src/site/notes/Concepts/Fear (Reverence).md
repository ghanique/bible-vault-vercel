#concept

There are two kinds of "fear":

- the [[Concepts/Fear (Afraid)\|Fear]] of being afraid, and
- the [[Concepts/Fear (Reverence)\|Fear]] of God, which is an attitude of reverence and respect.

This note is about the latter.
